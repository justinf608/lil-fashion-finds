What We Accept

  • Clean, gently used clothing in good condition
  • Current styles & classic staples (no older than ~5 years)
  • Shoes and boots (clean soles, no major wear)
  • Handbags, scarves, belts, and accessories
  • All sizes welcome -- kids' through plus sizes
  • Seasonal items (we may hold off-season items until the right time)
All items must be freshly cleaned before drop-off. Give us a call to check availability -- we want to give your pieces the attention they deserve!

Our Story

A little shop with a big heart

Lil' Fashion Finds & Consigns started with a simple idea: good clothes shouldn't go to waste, and great style shouldn't cost a fortune. We're a small, family-friendly shop right here in Edgerton, Wisconsin -- and we believe getting dressed should feel like an adventure, not a chore.

Every item on our racks has a story. Someone loved it, wore it, and now it's ready for its next chapter -- with you. We love the thrill of the hunt, the joy of an unexpected find, and the warm feeling of knowing your purchase kept something out of a landfill.

We're proud to be part of this community and grateful for every customer who walks through our door. Whether you're a seasoned thrifter or brand new to secondhand shopping, you're always welcome here. Come say hi -- we can't wait to help you find something wonderful.

Why shop with us?

Kinder to your wallet

Designer and quality pieces at a fraction of retail price. More finds for fewer dollars.

Kinder to the planet

Every item you buy here is one less thing in a landfill. Fashion can be fun and responsible.

Full of surprises

Every visit is different. You never know what treasure is waiting on the rack -- that's half the fun!

FAQ

Questions? We've got answers!

Everything you might be wondering before your first visit.

What kinds of items do you carry?
We carry gently used clothing for women, men, and kids -- plus shoes, bags, scarves, belts, and accessories. Our inventory is always rotating, so there's something new to discover every visit. We're especially well stocked with kids' items and love carrying unique, hard-to-find things like dress-up clothes, specialty outerwear, and fun seasonal finds.
How do the monthly sales work?
We run a handful of recurring sales throughout the month -- things like Half-Off Mondays, a Fill-a-Bag Sale, a Senior Discount Day, and a New Tag Color Drop. Check the Monthly Sales section above for the current schedule. Sales rotate and update, so it's always worth checking before you come in!
Can I drop off items for the shop?
Yes! We love receiving gently used items in good condition. All items must be freshly cleaned before drop-off. Give us a call or message us on Facebook before bringing in a large batch so we can make sure we're ready for you. Check the Drop Off section above for a list of what we're currently looking for.
What are your hours?
Our hours vary week to week, and we post a fresh schedule each month on our Facebook page -- that's the best place to check before stopping in! Typically we're open afternoons on weekdays and mornings on weekends, with some extended evening hours for special events.
What payment do you accept?
We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards. Questions? Give us a call and we'll be happy to help!
Where exactly are you located?
We're at 22 West Fulton Street, Edgerton, WI 53534. Check the map in the Visit Us section, or give us a call and we'll help you find us. We can't wait to meet you!
